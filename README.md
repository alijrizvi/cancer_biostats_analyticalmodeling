# Overview

### Credit for Guidance: Biostatsquid | https://www.youtube.com/@biostatsquid

This project focused on breast cancer diagnoses and key metrics surrounding patients to deeply analyze patient survival estimates, features and vital signs of morbidities and future outcomes, and predict the efficacies of various treatments (e.g., hormonal therapy and chemotherapy) toward breast cancer as well as average estimated survival times and probabilities.

## A Novel Addition

Furthermore, concepts and methodologies from Biostatistics were also applied in the form of, and not limited to:

* **Survival Curve** to depict the probability of death across years after diagnosis.
* **Kaplan-Meier Curve** graphed to show the results of a **Log Rank** test comparing the efficacy of Chemo or No Chemo.
*  **Cox Regression** implemented to estimate the effect of variables (covariates) (e.g., tumor size, no. of nodes, stage of cancer) on patient survivability, supplemented by interpreation of the resulting **Hazard Ratios**.
* Plotting the **Schoenfeld Residuals** over time, for each covariate.
